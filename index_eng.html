SASCHA GROM

BIO

Gromova Alexandra was born in 1992 in Kazan. She is a documentary photographer, graduated from the Kazan State Institute of Culture with a degree in theater director. Also, she graduated from the at school of modern photography “DocDocDoc”. Member of the Union of Photo Artists of Russia. Has published in such publications as Takie Dela, Dodho Magazine, The Calvert Journual, Zapovednik, etc.
